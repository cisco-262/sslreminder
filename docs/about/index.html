About SSL Reminder

SSL Reminder is a focused iPhone app for SSL certificate expiry reminders, built by OpsHome.

What SSL Reminder is

SSL Reminder helps developers, webmasters, and homelab users monitor public SSL/TLS certificate expiry dates. It keeps the product narrow on purpose: add certificate targets, review status, and receive push notifications before expiry.

Why it exists

Certificate expiry problems are small until they break a production site, API, dashboard, or personal service. SSL Reminder gives users a lightweight way to keep those dates visible without setting up a full monitoring system.

Part of the OpsHome product family

OpsHome builds tools for practical operations visibility. SSL Reminder covers focused SSL expiry reminders. OpsHome NOC covers broader uptime, private service monitoring, Docker Probe, Synology, Proxmox, Linux hosts, Docker containers, and homelab operations workflows.
