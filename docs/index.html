Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to use SSL Reminder?

No. SSL Reminder does not require sign-up. You can monitor your certificate targets without creating an account.

How many certificates can I monitor for free?

You can monitor up to 15 SSL certificate targets for free.

Does SSL Reminder monitor private network certificates?

Cloud monitoring is designed for public SSL certificates. Private LAN or VPN-only targets are not suitable for long-term cloud monitoring in SSL Reminder.

What happens when a certificate is about to expire?

SSL Reminder sends iPhone push notifications before expiry, including 14-day, 7-day, 1-day, and expiry-day reminders.
